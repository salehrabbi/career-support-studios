Why Engage Dr. Andrew Smith?

Integrate theory with practice.

Having taught at the undergraduate and graduate level for over 10 years, and been an entrepreneur and national security consultant for four years, Dr. Smith can uniquely introduce students to the theory and practice of Domestic and International Security Studies (e.g terrorism, counterterrorism, PVE/CVE/HVE) and Development (e.g. business development, proposal management and writing, project management, etc.).

Get timely, provocative insight.

Dr. Smith loves research and writing, and prides himself on developing usable and insightful analysis. With over 15 years of research and writing experience, Dr. Smith loves doing original field-research, action-research, and producing peer-reviewed research and columns on topics related to terrorism and counterrorism, domestic and international politics, American cultural affairs, and entrepreneurship.

Let’s solve real-world problems.

Dr. Smith loves strategizing to start and grow businesses informed by his five-years of entrepreneurial experience. Whether he is working with clients or partners to get clear on their primary aim, to designing and testing business models via business model canvases or minimally viable products, Dr. Smith enjoys working with small business owners to ascertain product market fit and solving the real-world problems of business owners and customers.

Standards and operating procedures save time and money.

From owning his own business to working with nonprofits, creating frameworks to communicate to new hires or implement projects on “How we do business here” has become increasingly apparent to maximize efficiencies. Work with Dr. Smith to standardized and potentially automate your operations.

ABOUT me

Beyond Dr. Smith’s professional capacity to integrate theory with practice as a professor, provide timely and provocative insight as an analyst, solve real-world problems for entrepreneurs and their customers, and the value that he places on integrating standards into businesses to increase efficiencies, Dr. Smith enjoys inspiring, empowering and serving others. He’s found that when you help others identify their passion, and provide them with the resources and tools to solve their own problems, something happens, irreducible to typed copy.
